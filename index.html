FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

🕐 How long does a typical project take?

Basic websites take 2–4 days. Full-featured web apps or landing pages can take up to a week, depending on complexity.

🔑 Do you build websites for businesses or just portfolios?

Absolutely. I create landing pages, business sites, and personal portfolios — all tailored to your goals.

💰 How much does it cost to build a website?

A basic personal or business website typically starts around ₹2000–₹5000. Larger or more customized projects like login systems, landing pages, or multi-page sites may cost more depending on features. Every project is different, so I offer fair pricing based on you exact needs.

💸 How do you accept payments?

Payments are made in two parts: 30% upfront and 70% after deployment. I accept UPI, PayPal, or direct bank transfer.

💬 Do you work with clients who don’t know anything about websites?

Absolutely! I explain everything in simple language and handle all the technical stuff for you.

🌍 Can you connect my website to a domain like myname.com?

Yes, I can set up your custom domain and connect it to your hosted site with SSL security.

📱 Will my website work on mobile and desktop both?

Yes. Every website I build is 100% responsive and looks great on phones, tablets, and desktops.

🛠️ Will I get the source code and control after the project is done?

Yes. You’ll receive the full source code and admin access if needed, so you have complete control.

🔁 What if I need changes after the project is done?

I offer 2 free revisions after delivery. After that, small updates are affordable and fast.
